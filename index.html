Dropping out of university?
While studying, it is difficult to keep focus on subjects and exam preparation. Often one of the consequences of poor organization is not passing the subjects and, as a result, leaving the university.
Studying for the “degree”?
Many students do not enjoy learning process and often just study to get a degree. As one of the consequences, such people prepare only to pass the subject subjects and do not try to understand how useful could be this subject to them in the future.
Where to begin career?
Although universities offer a wide variety of elective subjects, not all students take electives in a certain direction. As a result, after listening to objects from different areas, they cannot determine which of the fields or subfields they would like to work.
No passion to work?
