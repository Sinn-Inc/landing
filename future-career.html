How to apply my knowledge

The learning time is the time when students try to gain as much knowledge and skills as possible to apply them in their future work. At the same time, you can go to industry after a bachelor's degree and after a master's degree, and all students, depending on their chosen specializations, have different strengths.

But how to understand what qualities of yours are really in demand in the industry and how to improve them to show your best side?

Solution

Let's get some work done

At university, students attend lectures and receive not only grades but also skills in certain programming languages and technologies.

To gain an understanding in which projects the student participated and which languages he used, we introduce badges. When registering, a profile is created. Upon successful completion of a subject, seminar or practical work, he is granted a badge for a certain skill.

Thus, during studying, students accumulate badges and can understand which technologies they liked the most to study them in detail. These badges can be perfectly compared with the published vacan—Åies on sites with works.

When fully qualified, the student understands that he is well suited for the vacancy and is a good candidate. If a student lacks some skills, then he can easily learn them with the help of online courses, that will be recommended. For students who already have knowledge of certain technology and want to prove it, we offer professional certifications.

Thus, students become more confident in their knowledge, and the industry receives a trained workforce.
